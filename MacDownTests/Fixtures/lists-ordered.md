# Ordered List Tests

3. Third item

## List Starting with Different Number

5. Fifth item

## List with All Ones

1. Third item (still numbered 3)

## List with Paragraphs

3. Third item

## List with Inline Formatting

1. **Bold** item
2. *Italic* item
3. `Code` item

## Tight vs Loose Lists

Tight list:
1. Item one
2. Item two
3. Item three

Loose list:

1. Item one

2. Item two

3. Item three
