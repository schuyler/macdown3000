## Inline Math

The Pythagorean theorem states that \( a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \) for right triangles.

Einstein's famous equation is \( E = mc^2 \).

## Display Math

The quadratic formula is:

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

## Integral

$$
\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$$

## Matrix

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## Greek Letters

Symbols like \( \alpha \), \( \beta \), \( \gamma \), and \( \Delta \) are common in mathematics.

## Sum and Product

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$

$$
\prod_{i=1}^{n} i = n!
$$

## Fractions and Subscripts

The formula \( \frac{x_1 + x_2}{2} \) calculates the average.
